About Dogleg Band

Dogleg is an American post-hardcore band formed in Detroit, Michigan in 2015. The Dogleg band consists of Alex Stoitsiadis (lead vocalist, rhythm guitarist), Chase Macinski (bass), Parker Grissom (guitar), and Jacob Hanlon (drums). Dogleg music blends post-hardcore intensity with pop punk melodies and emo emotional depth.

Dogleg Band History

Dogleg band origins began in late 2015 when Alex Stoitsiadis started as a solo project in Rochester Hills, Michigan. Seeking creative freedom, he began writing songs in his parents' basement. The name "Dogleg" comes from the Bear vs. Shark song "Broken Dog Leg." In 2016, Stoitsiadis moved to Ann Arbor to attend the University of Michigan, where he continued developing Dogleg music.

Dogleg band gained momentum with their self-titled EP in 2016, followed by "Remember Alderaan?" later that year. Parker Grissom joined full-time, and the band began playing shows at Metal Frat, a Michigan fraternity known for hosting emerging bands like Brave Bird, La Dispute, and Pity Sex.

Dogleg Album - Melee

Dogleg's debut album "Melee" was released on March 13, 2020, to widespread critical acclaim. The Dogleg Melee album showcases the band's signature sound, blending melodic pop punk with post-hardcore energy. Tracks include "Fox," "Kawasaki Backflip," and "Ganon Main."

Dogleg Tour and Live Shows

Dogleg band began touring Michigan in 2017 and expanded to Midwest cities like Chicago, Indianapolis, and Akron in 2018. Dogleg represented Michigan at South by Southwest in Austin, Texas in 2018. The band was scheduled for a North America tour in March/April 2022 supporting Touche Amore and Vein.fm.

Dogleg live shows are known for their energetic performances and connection with fans. The band frequently played at Metal Frat fraternity in Michigan, building a dedicated following in the college town scene.

Dogleg Band Hiatus

On November 15, 2021, Dogleg announced they would be entering a hiatus amid allegations of possessive behavior in relationships towards frontman Alex Stoitsiadis. Despite the hiatus, Dogleg music continues to resonate with fans worldwide.
